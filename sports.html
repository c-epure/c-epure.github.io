Sports

I am participating in 1 sport this year and it is rowing. To be more specific I am on the Novice Boys team at the Chicago Rowing Foundation or CRF if you want to shorten it. This will be my 1st year rowing, but it has been going incredibly well with my boat getting 2nd place at our 1st competiton within our event. However, I have done many other sports. A few that I have done so far are tennis, skiing, figure skating, jiu-jitsu, and a bit of golf too. A sport that I may be interested in learning more about is Formula 1 which is a form of motor racing of open-wheeled vehicles that are made specifically for Formula 1. There are also lots of funny moments like in 2025 at the Australian Grand Prix when Charles Leclerc was talking with his race engineer. Plus some good quotes from Formula 1 drivers, such as one of my favorites said by Ayrton Senna:

"If you no longer go for a gap that exists, you are no longer racing driver."

The CRF

The Chicago Rowing Foundation is a very successful rowing group with several different teams and a large amount of boats and equipement to ensure that the team as a whole is improving and winning.
